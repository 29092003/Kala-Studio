About Us

We are a team of passionate professional photographers and videographers based in Bangalore dedicated to Create,Capture and Captivate unique junctures. ​


We believe in relishing and treading along the path of life with utmost grace and warmth. Hence we are dedicated to bring a few pauses to your breathtaking occasions so they can be reviewed as fresh as today, in the years to come. ​


With an experience of more than 27 years, we have a history of capturing special moments to make even more special memories.Our service encapsulates shooting a wide array of events ranging across weddings, birthdays, corporate meetings, aerial photography, short films, and architecture.More than a thousand happy customers of ours bear a witness to our timely, cost effective and quality service and deliveries.
